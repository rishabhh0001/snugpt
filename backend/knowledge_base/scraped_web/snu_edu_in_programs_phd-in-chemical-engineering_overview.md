Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering
Overview
The Department of Chemical Engineering houses a vibrant research culture, with faculty leading pioneering work across various focus areas. These include sustainable energy through biomass conversion, precision technologies via microfluidics and microparticle self-assembly, and the design of smart systems through computational modeling of responsive polymers. Faculty also explore eco-friendly solutions with bio-based coatings and packaging films, develop high entropy materials for advanced applications, and drive efficiency through process intensification. Additional strengths include cutting-edge manomaterial synthesis and catalysis, environmentally responsible supercritical fluid extraction, and innovative membrane separation technologies. Collectively, these efforts reflect the department’s dedication to scientific excellence, sustainability, and the advancement of transformative chemical engineering solutions.
