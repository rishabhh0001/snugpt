 Work Experience 
Dr. Rakshit has more than ten years of international experience in teaching Executive MBA and Full-time MBA programs and customized programs for executives. Dr. Rakshit also works with some of the leading technology accelerators in Russia and Kazakhstan, helping them design learning modules for nascent ventures. Dr. Rakshit has extensive experience in consulting and working with international developmental agencies, academia, sovereign wealth funds, and public enterprises. He has been a Visiting Scholar at the Fuqua School of Business at Duke University. He has been a Visiting Assistant Professor of Economics at the Williams School of Commerce, Politics and Economics at Washington and Lee University, USA, and an Assistant Professor at the Graduate School of Business, Nazarbayev University.
. 
 Research Interests 
His current research focuses on failure, restart, and strategic pivot among technology ventures, and regulatory co-evolution in nascent industries. He is also interested in understanding institutional barriers in the market for entrepreneurial finance and entrepreneurial network dynamics
