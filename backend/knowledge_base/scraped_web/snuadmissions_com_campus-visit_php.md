Campus Visit
We strongly encourage you to visit our campus. 
Please come and experience for yourself where you will spend four very significant years of
 your life. Our green, aesthetic surroundings, the academic blocks, classrooms and labs, the
 1.5 lakh sq. feet sports infrastructure, the library, the hostels, the dining halls, the cricket
 grounds - each constituent of the campus has its own tale to tell. 
The tours take place from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM and departs from the Library
 Building. The tour comprises an outdoor walk (please carry headgear/umbrellas) through the
 Library Building, Academic Blocks, Laboratories, Cafeteria, Hostel Blocks, the Bank, Mini
 Mart, etc. to provide in-depth information about the campus.
To visit the University, please follow the map for directions (we suggest you avoid the google map directions)
