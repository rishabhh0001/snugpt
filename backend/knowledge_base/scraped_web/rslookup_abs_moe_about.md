About rslookup
 About rslookup rslookup is a platform to find resources that students of SNIoE might need in their day to day
 life. What is rslookup? rslookup is a platform to find resources
 such as documents, professor details, lab locations, amenities and much more that students of SNIoE
 might need in their day to day life. The goal of the site is to be your first stop when you need
 to know about anything in the college. How did rslookup get started? This project started as a personal passion project on Sep 27, 2023 after I missed an office
 hour with a professor because I couldn't find the location of the office. He did not have it
 in his email signature or in the course handout, nor is mentioned in the SNU website. So I
 went through each academic building to find his office but couldn't find it. I've always liked the idea of open and free software, given that almost everything needed to
 build a website is available for free and I've always wanted to leave a mark in the university
 that I spent 4 years in. Although its a very important information, it is not available in the college website. So I
 thought of creating a platform where students can find all the information they need in one
 place. Where's the data coming from? Initially, the vision for the project was to make the website crowd sourced, where students
 can add resources that they find useful. But due to the lack of time and resources, I had to
 think of another way to keep the data up to date. Where else to get the most accurate and up to date information than the Student Council? The
 Student Council is the governing body of the students in the college and they have access to
 all the information that students might need. After signing a Memorandum of Understanding with the 2024-25 Student Council, I was able to strike a deal with them to use the website as a
 platform to host all the resources that they initially was sharing with the students through linktree.
