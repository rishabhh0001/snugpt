 Biography 
Dr. Indresh Kumar Verma holds a Ph.D. in Design from IIT Guwahati. With a strong academic background, Dr. Indresh Kumar Verma has dedicated himself to teaching and research in the field of Design.
Dr. Indresh has a keen interest in User Experience Design, Cognitive workload, Gamification, Cognitive Ergonomics and Interaction Design, aiming to contribute to the advancement of knowledge in the field. He has taught various courses in User Experience Design, which includes Design Process and Thinking, Visual Ergonomics and HCI, User Centric Design etc. Dr. Verma has also guided undergraduate students in design projects and mentored them in research paper writing. Dr. Indresh is a life member of Indian Society of Ergonomic (ISE) and Interaction Design Foundation (IDF). Dr. Verma is also serving as reviewer for many reputed Peer Reviewed National and International Journals.
