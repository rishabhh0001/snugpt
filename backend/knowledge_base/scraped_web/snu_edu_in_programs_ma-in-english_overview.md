M.A. in English
Overview
The M.A. in English at SNU is designed to combine rigor with creativity. The pedagogy includes classroom lectures designed to draw students into the complexity of texts. The excellent student-teacher ratio enables a rigorous, multitiered, and varied program of writing that will include compulsory courses in academic writing, good writing for the general public, and writing narratives.
