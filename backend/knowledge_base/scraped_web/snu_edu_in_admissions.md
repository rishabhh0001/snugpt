Study at Shiv Nadar University
We select our undergraduate cohorts through a merit-based system that combines several criteria, which may include performance in national examinations (Board examinations, JEE, etc.), the Institution's Achievement Tests (Shiv Nadar Institution of Eminence SAT and APT), internationally conducted tests such as the ACT and the SAT, and interviews conducted by faculty. The respective programs conduct selection and admissions to our master's and doctoral programs, and requirements vary accordingly.
 Why Shiv Nadar
The University's multidisciplinary education is built to yield capabilities for research and analysis, creative, problem-focused thinking, resilience, and respect for diversity. Our inspiring faculty is drawn from some of the best institutions in India and the world. The University has a 63,000 square feet Research Block and an on-campus incubation center to nurture entrepreneurial talent.
 What we look for
We are looking for students with a solid academic track record and demonstrate potential for combining 'soft' skills with a depth of knowledge in their chosen area of study. Our students typically have multiple capabilities ranging from excellence in sports, creative endeavors, entrepreneurial talent, or exceptional commitment to a social cause.
 What we offer
At the University, you discover yourself and become a part of a spectrum where several opportunities unfold. You become an essential part of this institution's journey that is envisaged to be an enduring global center of excellence in higher education - anchored in research and innovation, driven by outstanding faculty, and supported by national and international academic partnerships.

Scholarships
The University is committed to supporting deserving students in their educational journey. The University offers financial aid and scholarships to students selected through the specified admission criteria(s) in undergraduate programs, covering part or entire academic and living expenses.
Scholarships Offered:
The university offers scholarships to deserving students. The details are given below:
 Guidelines for Scholarship and Financial aid: 
- Applicants must select the type of scholarship they wish to apply for when they are filling the application form.
- Interested students must have to submit details in the scholarship form available on their dashboard.
- Shiv Nadar University, Delhi-NCR reserves the right to edit and alter this eligibility policy as it deems fit.
- Two scholarships cannot be held at the same time, a student must choose any one scholarship if awarded more than one scholarship.
- All decisions on scholarships rests solely with the university and is not subject to challenge or appeal.
- For any query, please write to [email protected]
 Categories of Scholarships: 
The following types of undergraduate scholarships are offered by the University:
Interdisciplinary Humanities & Social Sciences (IHS) Scholarships:- The top 10 performers securing admission to the B.A. (Research) in Interdisciplinary Humanities & Social Sciences (IHS) program through any entrance test will receive a 100% tuition fee waiver for all four years of study.
School Topper’s Scholarship:- Awarded to students who have achieved the highest academic standing in their respective schools in class 12th Board Exam.
Merit-Based Scholarship:- Granted to students with outstanding academic performance in qualifying examinations such as Class 12 Board or equivalent Exams.
JEE and UCEED Scholarship:- Awarded to students with extraordinary performance in the JEE (Main) and UCEED entrance exams.
Need-Based Scholarship:- Provided to students from low-income households to ensure access to quality education at Shiv Nadar University, Delhi NCR.
Sports Scholarship:- Awarded to students who have demonstrated excellence in sports at the international, national, or state level.
Women in B.Tech. Scholarship:- Designed to encourage and support female students pursuing undergraduate programs in Engineering and Technology.
Vice Chancellor’s Scholarship Granted to top-performing students in the University’s entrance examination (SNUSAT) across programs.
Armed Forces Dependents Scholarship:- Available to wards of serving, retired, or deceased personnel of the Indian Navy, Indian Air Force, Indian Coast Guard, and Indian Army.
Students who get selected through College Board SAT can apply for College Board India Scholarship which is awarded by the College Board to Indian students with exceptional SAT scores and demonstrated financial need. Please refer to College board website for Details. 
 Detailed terms and conditions of Scholarships: 
School Topper’s Scholarship : The student must be the topper of the school from which he/she completed the Class 12 Board. The school principal has to certify that the student is the topper of the school in 2026 Board examination. The minimum score required to be eligible for this scholarship is 98 % aggregate including all subjects listed in the Class 12th marksheet. Students selected for this scholarship may receive 100% waiver on tuition fee.
Merit-Based Scholarship – The minimum score required to be eligible for this scholarship is 95% aggregate including all subjects listed in the Class 12 marksheet (minimum 40 for IB and A* for cambridge board). Students selected for this scholarship may receive 50% waiver on tuition fee.
JEE (Main) Scholarship - Students who have secured admission through JEE (Main) and has scored more than 98 percentiles may be eligible for 100% tuition fee waiver, and students having scored between 95 to 98 percentiles in the JEE (Main) may be considered for 50% tuition fee waiver.
UCEED Scholarship - This is a special scholarship for students joining the four-year BDes program, The top UCEED performer may apply for this scholarship, students having ranked 120 and better may receive 100 % tuition fee waiver .
Need-Based Scholarship – Students with combined family income of less than ₹3 lakhs annually are eligible for this scholarship. ITR verification, home visit and personal interaction is conducted by the university through a third party for verification. Selected students may receive up to 100 % waiver on tuition fee as well as hostel fee.
Sports Scholarship – A student can apply for sports scholarships for the games/sports in Badminton, Chess, Squash, Table Tennis, Lawn Tennis, and any other sport recognized by the Department of Physical Education and Sports at Shiv Nadar University, Delhi-NCR. Students must have represented/won medal at International, National/School Nationals, State in respective sports/games recognized by National/International sports federations/bodies of respective sports/games. Students playing for India at recognized international tournaments from other sports, may also be considered for this scholarship as an exception and as per the availability of the required resources and, the number of players. Students selected for this scholarship may receive 20% to 100 % waiver on tuition fee depending on the decision of the sports committee.
Women in B.Tech. Scholarship – Only female students joining the engineering (B.Tech) program of the university are eligible. The performance in the entrance examination used for seeking admission to our BTech program would be used for deciding the eligibility for this scholarship. Students selected for this scholarship may receive ₹1 Lakh (One lakhs annually) waiver on tuition fee.
Vice-Chancellor’s Scholarship – Students selected for this scholarship may receive ₹1 Lakh (One lakhs annually) waiver on tuition fee depending on the performance in the entrance examination used for selection in the undergraduate program at Shiv Nadar University, Delhi-NCR.
Armed Forces Dependents Scholarship – Available to wards of serving, retired, or deceased personnel of the Indian Navy, Indian Air Force, and Indian Coast Guard and Indian Army. Students selected for this scholarship may receive 25-100% waiver on tuition fee.
 Duration of Scholarships: 
- All scholarships shall be awarded for one academic year.
- All scholarships are valid for 4 years of the program duration, provided the student satisfies the minimum CGPA criteria of 7.0, minimum attendance of 75 % in every semester (except for Sport Scholarships), and no disciplinary action against the applicant is taken or pending during the entire tenure of the program.
 Retaining Your Scholarship or Financial Aid: 
Students who receive scholarships or financial aid must maintain a CGPA of 7.0 at the end of every Spring Semester (after consideration of the Summer Semester, if any), minimum 75% of attendance (except sports scholarship), and no disciplinary action against the applicant is established or pending during the entire tenure of the program to retain their scholarships.
 Financial Aid 
Once you are accepted into the program you applied for, you will receive a form to provide additional details. The committee on financial aid will consider all your information and take a decision. The committee's decision will be final and binding.
