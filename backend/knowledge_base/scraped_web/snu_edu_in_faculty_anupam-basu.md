 Biography 
Prof. Anupam Basu is a professor at Department of Computer Science Engineering in IIT Kharagpur and currently a director of NIT, Durgapur. He has taught at University of California, Irvine, and University of Guelph, Ontario, Canada as visiting Professor and has been the Humboldt Fellow at TU Dortmund, Germany. He teaches Computer Architecture, Operating Systems, and Embedded Systems IIT. He has also conducted Artificial Intelligence, Intelligent Systems, and Cognitive Science courses at IITK.
Currently, he is Honorary Professor at CSE, SNU since 1st June 2021.
