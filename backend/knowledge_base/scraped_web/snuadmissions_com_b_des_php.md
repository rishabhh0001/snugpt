Bachelor of Design (B. Des.)
Bachelor of Design (B. Des) Program at SNIoE is a unique program envisaged
 to prepare Design leaders capable of addressing the multi-dimensional
 complex problems of the 21st century, contributing towards a more
 sustainable future. It adopts a Human-Centered approach to Design
 imparting trans-disciplinary education with a balance of courses in Social
 Sciences, Technology, and Design. Having mentoring from Industry and
 academia, the students get exposed to real-world problems through
 hands-on exercises, Industry projects, and mandatory internships with
 Industrial start-ups and NGOs. The 4-year program has choice-based
 streaming in Product System Design, Visual Communication, and Experience
 Design with an option for upgradation to a 5-year Integrated B. Des. –M. Des.
 degree. The program has been formulated to meet the requirements of
 society and Industry to ensure sustainable development at large.
