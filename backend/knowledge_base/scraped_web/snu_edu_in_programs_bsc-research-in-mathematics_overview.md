B.Sc. (Research) in Mathematics
Overview
The B.Sc. Mathematics (Research) degree program is distinct in that it incorporates both practical work and research, also allowing students to complete a thesis with the guidance of an advisor. This provides  students with the opportunity to gain hands-on experience and develop practical skills in addition to theoretical knowledge. Empowerment through rigorous learning and research in fundamental areas of mathematics and emerging technologies is at the core of the undergraduate programme. The program duration for our B.Sc. (Research) program is 6 to 12 semesters.
