THE OFFICE OF THE REGISTRAR
Location: D012 (D Block, Ground Floor)
The Office of the Registrar supports the students, faculty, and staff of the University by performing a wide spectrum of functions related to student services, keeping records, and academic administration. The Registrar is the University's nodal point for interfacing with the statutory bodies and the state/central governments. There are four members in the Office of the Registrar.
