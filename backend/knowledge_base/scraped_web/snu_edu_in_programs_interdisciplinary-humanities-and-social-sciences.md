BA (Research) Interdisciplinary Humanities and Social Sciences (IHS)
Overview
Shiv Nadar University is a leader in multidisciplinary and interdisciplinary education. We are proud to launch a new Interdisciplinary BA program in Humanities and Social Sciences, providing students with diverse specialization options. You can join this program regardless of your stream of study in high school. The world is opening up new opportunities every day. We will prepare you to grab such opportunities.
Sustainability Studies
Archaeology, Heritage, and Historical Studies
Society, Culture, Technology
All students in the program will share a common core curriculum in the first year of study to engage in diverse areas of expertise such as management, social and natural sciences, A.I., ethics, environment and writing and commuication skills. At the start of the second year, students can choose an interdisciplinary area of specialisation and move on to pursue the courses designed to enhance students’ knowledge and experiential learning.
 
Scholarships
10 scholarships per batch will be awarded to eligible students. The scholarships cover 100% tuition waiver for all four years!
