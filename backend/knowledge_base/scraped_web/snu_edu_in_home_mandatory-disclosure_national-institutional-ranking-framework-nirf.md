National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF)
Shiv Nadar University has been the youngest institution in the 'top 100' Overall list in the Ministry's National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF). Shiv Nadar University, Delhi-NCR was ranked at 56 in the ‘University’ category in the National Institute Ranking Framework (NIRF), published by the Ministry of Education, Govt. of India. The University is also among a selected group of green-field institutions in the country which were awarded the prestigious Atal Incubation Center grant by the Niti Aayog, Government of India, in the very first round in 2017.
