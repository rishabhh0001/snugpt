Board of Management
The Board of Management (BOM) is the principal executive body of Shiv Nadar (Institution of Eminence Deemed to be University) in accordance with the provisions of the Rules and By-Laws of the Shiv Nadar IOE. The BOM approves the budget of the Institution, the establishment of new schools, programs, all matters related to admission, fees and scholarship policies of the Institution.
