 About Atal Incubation Centre (AIC) 
AIC at Shiv Nadar University, Delhi NCR is a tech agnostic hardware-focused premier incubation center established in 2017 with the support of AIM Niti Aayog to foster the culture of innovation and entrepreneurship across the length and breadth of the country.
We aim to create a holistic environment to help innovation-driven individuals to layout and succeed in their business ventures under coordinated direction. The incubated startups are not only an advantage because of the business resources and technical assistance, but they also benefit from a supportive and conductive community with vibrant academia who lead and inspire them to build a sustainable business model that stands out in the competitive environment.
