 Education 
Dr. Kathuria has an undergraduate degree in Economics from St. Stephen's College (University of Delhi), a Masters from the Delhi School of Economics (University of Delhi) and a PhD from the University of Maryland (College Park), USA. 
 Work Experience 
Spent over 12 years with the International Management Institute (IMI), New Delhi, teaching post-graduate students Managerial Economics and International Trade. He has also previously worked with the World Bank in Washington D.C. as a Consultant, and worked on research assignments for a number of international organizations, including the International Labour Organization (ILO), United Nations Conference on Trade and Development (UNCTAD), LIRNEasia, The World Bank and The Asian Development Bank (ADB).
