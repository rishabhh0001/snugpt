B.Sc. (Research) in Economics and Finance
The B.Sc. (Research) in Economics and Finance (BSEF) is offered jointly by the School of Management and Entrepreneurship (SME) and the School of Humanities and Social Sciences (SHSS). This is an interdisciplinary program designed to leverage the linkages between economic theory and the challenge of management of financial markets. The strengths of the departments of Finance, Accounting and Control in SME and Economics in SHSS combine in training and preparing students of BSEF to succeed in the dynamic and competitive world of Currencies, Money, Risk, and Finance.
The curriculum has been carefully planned keeping in mind the increasing need, relevance and application of the fundamental theories of economics and finance to the rapidly changing world of money and technology. The BSEF program equips students with a broad, as well as a deep understanding of the profound connections between the structure of economies and the functioning of markets and institutions in general, and with capital markets and financial institutions in particular.
