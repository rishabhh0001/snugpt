Instructions:
- The “Financial Aid Application Form” will be enabled on the application dashboard of all the students, the applicant must be informed to check the box “Financial Aid Required” while filling up the admission application form at the beginning of the admission process.
- Shiv Nadar University, Delhi-NCR reserves the right to edit and alter the eligibility criteria as it deems fit.
- Scholarship and Financial Aid cannot be clubbed, a student must accept any one of these if awarded.
- Details and proofs submitted with the Financial Aid Application Form will be physically verified by a nominated person at your address. you are advised to cooperate with the verification process.
- The decision to award the scholarship and financial aid rests solely with the university and is not subject to challenge or appeal.
- For any query, please write to [email protected]
All applicants are required to submit the following documents with their “Financial Aid” application form:
Declaration of the number of family and earning members
Declaration of the details of loan taken and loan statements for 6 months
Income Tax Returns of all earning members of the family for two years (recent)
Address Proof ( any two of the following: Voter ID, Ration card, Aadhar Card, Passport, Electricity bill)
Bank Account Statements of all earning family members for 6 months or Form 26 B of Mother and Father/ Earning members
Declaration by the parents on the number of bank accounts holds by them
School Fee Receipt(s) of the applicant for Class 12 and School Fee Receipt(s) for the applicant’s sibling/s.
Declaration of Details of Financial Asset(s) owned by the family as defined above
Company's Income Tax Returns and Bank Statements (in case any earning member of the family owns a Business)
Area in square feet (In case of houses and commercial spaces) and hectares (In case of agricultural land)
Declaration of the quantity of gold and silver in the possession of the immediate family members
Declaration of the vehicle/s owned by the immediate family members (copy of the registration certificate to be submitted).
Auto Loan if taken, the proof of the same to be presented.
Declaration of the value of the investments and proof document of Insurance, FD, Bonds, Mutual Funds, Insurance, and any other financial asset.
Declaration of the scholarship and financial aid received by any of the family members in the last 5 years.
Retaining Your Financial Aid:
Students who receive scholarships or financial aid must maintain a CGPA of 7.0 at the end of every Spring Semester (after consideration of the Summer Semester, if any), minimum 75% of attendance (except sports scholarship), and no disciplinary action against the applicant is established or pending during the entire tenure of the program to retain their scholarships.
