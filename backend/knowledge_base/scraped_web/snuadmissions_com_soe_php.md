SCHOOL OF ENGINEERING (SoE)
School of Engineering with innovative and engaging teaching methods aims to provide a world-class learning experience. It combines research and education at the undergraduate level, providing students a well-rounded experience. Each program at the school has been carefully developed in consultation with industry and academia to provide the best mix of theoretical and practical knowledge.
The courses offered fall in the following categories: University wide electives (UWE); Core Common Curriculum (CCC); Research, Experiential and Applied Learning (REAL); and Values, Ethics, Leadership and Service (VELS). The University is currently registered with DSIR, Government of India as a Research Institution. There are 54 state-of-the-art laboratories in SoE.
