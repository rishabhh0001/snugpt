B.Tech. in Electrical and Computer Engineering
The Department of Electrical Engineering is focused on imparting quality education through its well-structured undergraduate and doctoral programs. The undergraduate program "Electrical and Computer Engineering is based on the pedagogy of "learning by doing". The taught courses have relevant laboratory and hands-on component. These courses are categorized as (a) mandatory core courses and (b) elective courses, that students can choose based on their interests. Students may earn a specialization within the department based on their choice of elective courses. In addition, they may also earn minors in other disciplines of the university.
