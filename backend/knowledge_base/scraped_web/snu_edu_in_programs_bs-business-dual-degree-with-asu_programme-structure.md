BS Business (Dual Degree with ASU)
The BS in Business program at SNU offers a comprehensive 4-year undergraduate education, enriched with ASU curriculum. In addition, the integration of ASU courses in this program provides you the opportunity to accrue credits that can be transferred to ASU at the onset of your 3rd year, allowing you to complete both an ASU degree and a SNU degree simultaneously, in the same timeframe as one.

Eligibility & Transfer Requirements
Bachelor of Science (BS) - Business 
Eligibility
 
Minimum 65% marks in aggregate in Class 12th Board exam. Must include English.
Transfer Requirements to ASU after successful completion of first two years at Shiv Nadar University, Delhi NCR:
Minimum GPA of 6 out 10 as per Shiv Nadar University grading system applicable.
Other essential requirements for transfer
 
1230 SAT Reasoning OR
25 ACT Score, OR
3.4 GPA on a scale of 4 in ASU competency courses in high school
English Language Proficiency Requirement: English proficiency tests with minimum scores must have been taken no longer than two years before the student's start date at ASU. Any one of the following:
 
A minimum TOEFL iBT score of 61 (internet-based test, taken in a testing center)
A minimum IELTS score of 6
A minimum PTE score of 53
A minimum Duolingo English score of 95
