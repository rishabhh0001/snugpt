Overview
The Modelling and Simulation specialisation equips undergraduate students with vital computational and analytical tools to tackle complex chemical engineering challenges. By combining core engineering principles with modern simulation techniques, this specialisation enables students to model and analyse physical, chemical, and biological systems across multiple scales from molecular interactions to industrial processes. It offers a strong foundation for those interested in data-driven problem solving, process optimisation, and research in emerging areas of chemical engineering. As part of the specialisation, students will have to complete the following electives in the respective semesters.
Introduction to modelling and simulation
Computational fluid dynamics
Modelling and simulation of engineering systems
Molecular simulation
