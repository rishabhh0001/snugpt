Ph.D. in Economics
Overview
All full-time Ph.D. students admitted into the program shall receive a doctoral award (teaching and research assistantship) consisting of a tuition and hostel fee waiver of Rs. 60,000 each and a monthly stipend of ₹45,000 for the first two years and ₹50,000 for the next three years. The continuation of the award is subject to satisfactory performance in the program, evaluated continuously, and compliance with all University regulations. Financial support is available for Ph.D. students to disseminate their work through conferences.
Eligibility Criteria
Applicants must have either:
A minimum of 55% aggregate marks in any postgraduate degree program, or
A minimum of 75% aggregate marks in any four-year undergraduate degree program.
