Educational Resources
 
Educational Resources at Shiv Nadar (Institution of Eminence Deemed to be University)
Office 365
Office 365 is a cloud-based subscription service that brings together a variety of Microsoft applications delivered through their platform.
MS Teams
Microsoft Teams is a collaboration app that helps your team stay organized and have conversations—all in one place.
Google Suite for Education
G-suite is a package of multiple productivity tools for classroom collaboration which can be accessed from any web browser.
Blackboard - LMS
Shiv Nadar University uses Blackboard as its Learning Management System, a web-based learning management system (LMS).
Advisor Advisee Platform - SNU ERP
Advisor-Advisee module has been customized on SNU ERP (PeopleSoft Campus Solution) to assist students in their growth.
Grade Submission System
This system is designed for faculty to input course-wise grades of the students.
Course Evaluation Survey
This survey enables students to provide feedback on the course structure, faculty, and the way the course is conducted.
Course Offering System
This application automates all course offering related processes at Shiv Nadar University.
Student Attendance (SAMS)
The Student Attendance Management System allows faculty to record or maintain attendance of students.
HPCC - Magus
‘Magus’ is SNU’s High Performance Computing cluster, designed to facilitate the computing needs of research projects.
Urkund
URKUND offers a fully-automated e-mail based Plagiarism check system for students, faculty, and research scholars.
Koha - e-Library
Shiv Nadar University uses Koha as its e-Library, an open-source Integrated Library System (ILS).
VDI - Virtual Desktop Infrastructure
SNU has piloted VDI deployment using VMware's Horizon solution, a leading platform for virtual desktops and applications.
E-learning
Shiv Nadar University has an intranet-based video platform for e-learning consisting of over thirteen thousand video lectures.
24X7 Study Areas
SNU Campus has four 24X7 Study areas with wired Internet connectivity: Library (E-Wing), Hostel 1 A, Hostel 2A.
Impartus Lecture Capture
Impartus Lecture Capture solution provides a powerful and stable video learning platform equipped with rich features.
