M.Sc. in Economics
The Department of Economics at the Shiv Nadar Institution of Eminence has released the initial notification for admission to the M.Sc. (Economics) program for the academic year 2026–27, along with the updated selection criteria.
The two-year Master's program in economics is a top-tier postgraduate program in economics in India. The admitted students will receive training within an active research environment supported by faculty members working at the forefront of research in their respective fields.
