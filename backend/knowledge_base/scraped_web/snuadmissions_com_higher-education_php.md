HIGHER EDUCATION
Shiv Nadar University students are immensely successful in their pursuit of higher studies. They go to some of the finest institutions in the world – in India and abroad. Our programs, which are multidisciplinary, research-focused and rich in experiential education prepare you very well for that. The perfect way to learn about these opportunities is to speak to your professors who will guide you every step of the way. You can also connect to our alumni who are already admitted to some of the top universities in the world. 
The Career Development Center (CDC) provides support with the application process as well as alumni connections. 
During your study at Shiv Nadar, you can study abroad for a semester or do research.
Contact Harshita Tripathi Harshita.tripathi@snu.edu.in for more information
