 Research Interest 
Ultrafast spin, charge, and phonon dynamics studied using femtosecond optical pump–probe techniques.
Brillouin light-scattering spectro-microscopy of spin-wave dispersion and propagation in advanced magnetic structures and devices.
Broadband and spin-torque ferromagnetic resonance investigations of spin waves and pure spin current.
Magnonics: from conventional approaches to hybrid and quantum magnonic systems.
Spin-orbitronics: pure spin currents, spin Hall effect, spin pumping, Dzyaloshinskii–Moriya interaction, and perpendicular magnetic anisotropy.
Spintronics with quantum materials.
Magnetic straintronics.
Spin-texture-driven magnonics and spintronics, including magnetic domain walls, vortices, skyrmions, and artificial spin ice.
THz time-domain spectroscopy of thin films and nanostructures.
Dielectric relaxation spectroscopy of binary mixtures.
Coherent control of ultrafast processes via optical pulse shaping.
