# Shiv Nadar University (SNU)

Shiv Nadar Institution of Eminence is a comprehensive, multidisciplinary, research-focused, and student-centric University.

## Admissions
The admission process is currently open. You can apply at snuadmissions.com. 
The university offers various undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs.

## Academics
The university has a strong focus on research and multidisciplinary learning.

## Campus Life
The campus is fully residential with modern hostel facilities, library, and sports complexes.

## IT Support
For Wi-Fi access, use your SNU NetID.
